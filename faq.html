Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

These are a list of the questions we receive a lot. If you have other questions, please contact us!

  • How is it that you don't charge any fees?

    We are able to offer fee-free payments because of the way our network works. Because our network costs are very low, we cover the costs for our merchants and users.

  • How does your company make money?

    Our business plan does not include charging fees to our users, or to merchants in our network. Our revenue plans are still very top secret, as it's a never-before-seen model, but rest-assured we do not plan to charge fees.

  • Is my money insured against loss/theft?

    All funds in the system are stored in traditional commercial bank accounts, insured by the FDIC up to $250,000.

  • Is Roam's payment network safe/secure?

    Yes. We use the same encryption methods as banks and militaries to store information in an immutable database. That means records can not be changed or erased, only added to. If any theft were to occur, it would be easy for us to track.

  • What happens if I lose my phone?

    Because your account is tied to your phone number, nothing is stored on your phone (no apps!). That means that if you lose your phone, your money will be waiting for you when you get a new one. In short, nothing. Except that you lost your phone :(

  • How does it all work?

    Roam uses a unique "no-app" approach to sending money. We do this by running a free text service that sends money for you. All you have to do is text our number and tell us what you want to do! This is important because it's easy, works on all phones with text messaging, does not require a data plan, and grandma can do it ;)

  • What stores are in your network?

    We are currently in a testing phase to make sure that our product makes sense to people. For that reason, we are not open to new users at the moment and we have only 3 stores in our current test.

Did we miss something?

If you have any questions which are not answered here, or just need further calrification on any of these answers - please contact us!

Roam is a fast, efficient payment network for the 21st century. Send money over a text message, in seconds, with the security of a bank.
