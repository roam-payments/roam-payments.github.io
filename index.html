Get Started

No Fees

We don't charge any fees for you to send and receive money. You've worked hard for your money, keep it.

Data Privacy

No bank accounts, and no passwords. Whatever data we store about you is private, and not for sale.

Instant Payment

No more waiting 3-5 days for money to transfer. Our payment network transfers money in 3-5 seconds.

Safe and Secure

We use bank-grade security, and all funds are held in FDIC-insured accounts in traditional banks.

Change is hard, but texting is easy.

Why join our merchant program?
