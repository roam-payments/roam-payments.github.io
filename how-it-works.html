About Us

How we were founded

Easy, fee-free payments for individuals and businesses

After seeing businesses in our small town struggle with credit card fees and increased competition from online shopping, we decided to come up with a solution. A new network - for the 21st century. Using state-of-the-art cryptography for bank-grade security, and packing into a simple interface...we can move money fast, without fees, as easy as sending a text message.

No more sending money at the speed of 3-5 business days. Let's take advantage of the speed and efficiency of the internet.
