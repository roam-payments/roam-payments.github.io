Get Started

Getting started with Roam is easy

The "No-app" approach

Works on every phone. iOS, Android, Smartphone or not

We decided to really simplify things. We thought to ourselves, "why can't money move like a text message?" - and that's when it hit us!

To get started, text 'begin' to (307) 461-9903

Pro-tip: Don't forget to save us as a contact in your phone!

Other options

Simple design

Just text 'list' to our number after you sign up to see the available options. For example, you can text the command below to send us a dollar.

send $1 3027512344

Pro-tip: You can send to any number. If they don't have an account, we will create one for them and send the money their way!

Roam is a fast, efficient payment network for the 21st century. Send money over a text message, in seconds, with the security of a bank.
